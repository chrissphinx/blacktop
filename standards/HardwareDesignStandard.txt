Use KiCAD 5 for board design.

Use the default KiCAD design rules for feature size and separation.

Instructions for board design in KiCAD 5 are found in Contextual Electronics' "Getting To Blinky 5.0" on YouTube:
https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLy2022BX6EspFAKBCgRuEuzapuz_4aJCn

Schematics must pass KiCAD's Electric Rules Check (ERC) with no errors or warnings.

Layouts must pass KiCAD's Design Rules Check (DRC) with no errors or warnings.
